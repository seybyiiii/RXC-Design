GRAPHICS

Poster

This series is the result of a few years spent experimenting with different styles and techniques. Each poster gave me a chance to test out a new typographic hierarchy or a specific color theory, pushing me to find that sweet spot between striking palettes and clean composition.

Looking back, these designs really show how my approach to visual storytelling has evolved.

Chibi

A digital illustration collection where I made chibi designs from head-to-bust icons. I love the challenge of making a character instantly recognizable in a tiny, polished package.

I prioritize bold outlines and expressive eye details to ensure each piece works perfectly as a profile icon or a standalone character design.

Vector

A collection which features clean and minimal linework to turn photos into a timeless vector illustration that focus on memory.

These pieces are all about using simple outlines to capture a specific moment and the connection between the people in it.
