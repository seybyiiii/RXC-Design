Web

For this project, I focused on creating a clean, professional web presence for a traditional Filipino spa. It's a custom-built website featuring a full service menu and an integrated booking platform. I designed the site to reflect the spa's serene atmosphere, using a minimalist aesthetic that highlights their traditional healing techniques.

It was important that the site worked perfectly on any device, but the real goal was the atmosphere. I aimed to create a clean, accessible space that mirrors the relaxing environment the spa provides for its clients.
